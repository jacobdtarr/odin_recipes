Lasagna Recipe

The humble favourite of Garfield origniated in the Tuscan region of southern Italy where an Olive tree was planted in the year 1001 because they wanted to.

The recipe is simple. You make the sauce and then you put it between pasta layers and bam top with cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Cottage chesse
  • Shredded chesse blend
  • Rectangle pasta

Recipe

  1. Cook ground beef and stir with pasta sauce
  2. Lay down a layer of rectangle pasta on bottom of deep cooking dish
  3. Put pasta sauce blend and cottage cheese on top of pasta and put another layer on top
  4. Repeat until dish full
  5. Top with shredded cheese blends and bake at 375F for 1.5hrs
